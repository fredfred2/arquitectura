  • When you click the Delete button, the specific item will get deleted and page gets reloaded
  • When you click the Refresh button, the page gets submitted but the items that were deleted still does not appear.
  • When you click the Home button and then get back to this page, the items will remain as in the previous state.
  • When you click the End button, the conversation scope comes to an end. When you visit this page, the original set of items are displayed.
